Pork ribs with coleslaw

Ingredients for

4 Pork ribs
4 Packs jerk or BBQ seasoning
80 g Brown sugar
400 ml Ketschup
400 ml BBQ-Sauce
400 g Cabbage
200 g Carrots
8 table spoon Mayonaise
4 Garlich cloves
Salt and pepper to taste

Ingredients

Preheat the oven to 200°C. Prepare the pork ribs: Turn your pork ribs upside down, make a small cut underneath the membrane covering the bones on the back of the ribs. Pull firmly and remove the membrane. Mix together the brown sugar, the BBQ/jerk-seasoning, some salt and peper. Season the ribs on both sizes with your seasoning mixture. Wrap the seasoned ribs in a backing sheet and wrap them again in aluminium foil. Place the wrapped ribs on a backing tray and put them in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.

After 30 minutes take the ribs out of the oven and take them out from the aluminium foil and the backing sheet. Mix together the ketchup and the BBQ-sauce and brush the ribs with your sauce mixture. Place the ribs back on the backing tray. Reduce the oven's heat to 170°C and cook the ribs for another 1,5 hours. Every 30 minutes take the ribs out of the oven and brush them again with your sauce mixture. After the baking time, take the ribs out of the oven and let them cool on a plate. Put your potato chips on the backing tray and bake them for 15 minutes.

Prepare the coleslaw: Grate the cabbage, carrots and garlic, mix them with mayo salt and pepper. Arrange everything nicely on your plate and enjoy with a beer. Cheers!
