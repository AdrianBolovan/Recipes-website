Swedisch Köttbullar

Ingredients for

Köttbullar
1,5 dl Water
0,75 dl Breadcrumbs
0,5 dc Yellow onion
500 gr Minced beef
400 ml BBQ-Sauce
1 Egg
200 g Carrots
3 ts Concentrated veal stock
Salt
Black pepper
Butter
Cream souce
2 dl Water
1 dl Whipped cream
2 ts Concentrated veal stock
1 sts Soja souce
1 ts Honey
Black pepper
2 ts Cornstarch
2 ts Cold water

Ingredients

Mix the water and breadcrumbs in a bowl and let rest for about 5 minutes. Peel and finely chop the onion. Mix the mincemeat into the breadcrumb mixture and add the onion, egg and veal stock. Season with salt and black pepper. Shape the mince into meatballs. Fry the meatballs in butter in a frying pan until nicely colored and cooked through, 8-10 minutes.

Cream Sauce: Mix the water, cream, veal stock and soy sauce in a saucepan. Boil and simmer for about 7 minutes. Season with honey and black pepper. Mix the cornstarch into the water in a glass and add the mixture to the sauce. Let it come to the boil.

Köttbullar is traditionally served with mashed potatoes.
