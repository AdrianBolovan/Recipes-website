Granny's Keiserschmarrn

Ingredients for

250 ml Milk
120 g Flour
3 Yolk
30 g Sugar
Salt
40 g Butter
3 Eggs

Ingredients

Mix the milk well with the flour and gradually stir in the egg yolks, sugar and a little salt as well as 40g of melted butter and the beaten egg whites.
Heat 60g butter in a pan, pour in the batter and place in the hot oven for 25 minutes.
Tear the finished Kaiserschmarrn with two forks and allow to rest for a while.
