Turkish Iskender

Ingredients for

1 p Onion
1 p Garlic clove
4 g Oriental spice mix
5 g Mint
3 p Tomato
300 g Ground beef
10 g Butter
1 g Sweet paprika powder

Ingredients

Preheat the oven to 220 °C top/bottom heat (200 °C fan). Halve the tomatoes, remove the stems, and roughly chop them. Peel the onion, cut in half, and slice into strips. Peel the garlic and slice half of it.

Cut the flatbread in half and then into approx. 2 cm cubes. Place the bread cubes on a wire rack and bake above the tomatoes for about 10–12 minutes until toasted. Finely chop parsley and mint leaves separately. In a small bowl, mix yogurt with the chopped mint and season with salt and pepper.
